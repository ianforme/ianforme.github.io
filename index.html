Hi there, I'm Sun Weiran!

I graduated from National University of Singapore in 2018 with a distinction bachelor degree in Business Analytics (Honours). Currently I am working as a Data Scientist in NCS Singapore, focusing on delivering both operational and prove-of-concept data science projects to major local clients such as Changi Airport and Land Transport Authority (LTA). I have successful experiences in predictive modeling, machine learning, data visualisation, data pipeling and analysis. I am strong in Python, SQL and Tableau.

Some of my past and active projects are:

  • Flight Passenger Load Prediction (Airport/POC) - improved prediction accuracy from existing solution by ~20%
  • Future Taxi Demand Prediction (Airport/Operational) - customized and robust live predictive engine with ~80% accuracy
  • Road Traffic Speed and Volume Prediction (LTA/Operational) - end-to-end data pipeline and Tableau dashboard visualisation

Believing in sharing and open-source, I am a contributing writer of Towards Data Science and author of code encryption Python library Codimage.
