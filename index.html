  • Future Taxi Demand Prediction (Airport/Operational) - Customized and robust live predictive engine with ~80% accuracy
  • Road Traffic Speed and Volume Prediction (LTA/Operational) - End-to-end data pipeline and Tableau dashboard visualisation

I have proven and successful experiences in predictive modeling, machine learning, data analysis and visualization. With good understanding of various data science algorithms, I am able to engineer and deliver in Python, SQL and Tableau. Believing in sharing and open-source, I am a contributing writer of Towards Data Science and the author of code encryption Python library Codimage.
